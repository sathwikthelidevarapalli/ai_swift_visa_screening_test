 Basic Eligibility Requirements - Both the applicant and the partner must be **aged 18 years or older**
 Relationship and Genuine Intent Requirements - The relationship must be **genuine and subsisting**
 Financial Requirements - The sponsor must meet the **minimum income threshold** of **£18,600 per year**
 - Additional financial requirements apply if dependent children are included
 English Language Requirement - The applicant must prove **English language ability** at **A1 level or higher** of the Common European Framework (CEFR) through: • An approved English language test, OR • A degree taught in English, OR • Citizenship from a majority English-speaking country
 Accommodation Requirements - The couple must have **adequate accommodation** available in the UK that meets public health and overcrowding standards
 Confirm eligibility and gather relationship, financial, and accommodation evidence
 - Financial documents (payslips, bank statements, tax records)
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants may be refused if they: - Fail to prove a genuine and subsisting relationship
 - Do not meet the financial or language requirements
