 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must be invited by a U
 - The applicant must have the background and qualifications appropriate for the training opportunity offered
 Specific Eligibility – Special Education Exchange Visitor - The applicant must either have completed or be pursuing a bachelor’s or higher degree in special education, **or** have extensive practical experience in the field
 Employer files **Form I-129, Petition for a Nonimmigrant Worker**, with USCIS, along with the training description and proof of eligibility
 Key Documents Required - Form I-129, Petition for a Nonimmigrant Worker
 - Supporting materials for special education exchange programs, including facility credentials and staff qualifications
 Common Reasons for Ineligibility - Training program involves normal productive employment or lacks clear educational purpose
 - Applicant lacks relevant qualifications or background for the proposed training
