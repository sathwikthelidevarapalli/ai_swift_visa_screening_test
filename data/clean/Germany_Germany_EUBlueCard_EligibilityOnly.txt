 It is designed to attract skilled professionals with university degrees or equivalent qualifications to meet Germany’s labor market demands
 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must have a **valid job offer or employment contract** in Germany for a **highly-qualified position** lasting at least six months
 - The applicant must hold a **recognized university degree** or an equivalent higher-education qualification relevant to the position
 Employment and Qualification Requirements - The employment offer must be **for a qualified position**, typically requiring a degree-level education
 - Applicants in shortage occupations benefit from lower salary requirements
 - Qualifications obtained outside Germany must be either: • Recognized by the **Central Office for Foreign Education (ZAB)**, or • Deemed equivalent to a German university degree under the **Anabin database** system
 Financial and Legal Requirements - The applicant must **earn the minimum required annual gross salary** for eligibility
 - The employer must verify that the offered role matches the applicant’s qualifications
 Health and Security Requirements - The applicant must have **valid health insurance** coverage for the entire period of stay
 - The time requirement may be reduced to **21 months** if the holder demonstrates **B1-level German language proficiency**
 Collect required documentation, including proof of salary, qualifications, and health insurance
 - Proof of recognized qualifications (university degree or ZAB recognition)
 - Curriculum vitae (CV) and supporting professional documents
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants may be ineligible if they: - Do not meet the minimum salary requirement
 - Fail to prove qualification recognition or professional experience
