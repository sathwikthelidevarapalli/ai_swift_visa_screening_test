 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must qualify under at least **one of the three federal economic immigration programs**: • **Federal Skilled Worker Class (FSW)** – for individuals with skilled work experience abroad
 - The applicant must meet the **minimum requirements** for **language ability**, **education**, and **work experience**, specific to the chosen program
 - The applicant must **create an online Express Entry profile** and be accepted into the Express Entry pool by demonstrating eligibility under one of the three programs
 Minimum Program Requirements **1
 - Must hold a **valid job offer** for at least one year or a **certificate of qualification** issued by a Canadian provincial, territorial, or federal authority
 Documentation Requirements - Valid **passport or travel document**
 Admissibility Criteria - The applicant and accompanying family members must be **admissible to Canada**, including medical, criminal, and security checks
 Common Reasons for Ineligibility - Failure to meet program-specific requirements
 - Receiving an ITA does not guarantee permanent residence; applicants must submit complete documentation and meet all admissibility requirements
