 The visa supports family reunification and grants the applicant the right to live together with their spouse in Germany, subject to compliance with residence and integration requirements
 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The **applicant must be legally married** to: • A **German citizen**, or • A **foreign national holding a valid German residence permit or settlement permit**
 Language and Integration Requirements - The **applicant may be required to demonstrate basic German language proficiency (A1 level)** prior to visa issuance
 - Exemptions from this requirement may apply if: • The spouse in Germany is an EU/EEA citizen (excluding Germany)
 Financial and Accommodation Requirements - The **sponsor in Germany** must demonstrate: • **Sufficient income** to cover living costs of both spouses
 • **Adequate housing** that meets local space and safety requirements
 - The residence permit is typically valid for **the same duration as the sponsor’s permit** or, for spouses of German citizens, issued with long-term renewal eligibility
 Submit required documents proving marriage validity, income, and accommodation
 - Proof of sufficient financial means (salary slips, tax documents, employment contract)
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants may be found ineligible if they: - Cannot provide valid proof of marriage or relationship authenticity
 - Fail to meet language or financial requirements without a justified exemption
 - Submit incomplete or falsified documents
