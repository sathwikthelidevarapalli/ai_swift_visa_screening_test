 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must be **enrolled or accepted at a Designated Learning Institution (DLI)** in Canada
 Financial Requirements Applicants must provide proof of funds, which can include: - Proof of a Canadian bank account in the applicant’s name (if money has been transferred to Canada)
 The **minimum financial requirement** is based on IRCC’s annual cost-of-living estimates for international students and their dependents
 Common Reasons for Ineligibility - Failure to prove sufficient funds for tuition and living costs
