 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must apply under the **Skilled Worker route** for an occupation in an **eligible health or social care role**
 - The applicant must meet the **English language proficiency requirement** as defined under the Skilled Worker rules
 Financial and Maintenance Requirements - Applicants must demonstrate the ability to **support themselves financially** upon arrival unless their sponsor certifies maintenance on their behalf
 - Dependents (spouse/partner and children) may accompany the applicant if they meet the financial and eligibility conditions
 - Eligibility to work in the UK for up to **5 years**, with the option to extend or apply for **Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR)**
 Provide supporting documents including identity proof, CoS reference, and proof of English proficiency
 Ineligibility Conditions An application may be refused if the applicant: - Does not hold an eligible job offer or approved CoS
 - Fails to meet the salary or English language requirement
 - Does not meet suitability or admissibility requirements under the Immigration Rules
