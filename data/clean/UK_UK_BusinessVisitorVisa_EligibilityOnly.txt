 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must satisfy the **general eligibility conditions** for a UK Standard Visitor
 Financial and Documentation Requirements - The applicant must demonstrate access to **sufficient funds** to cover: • Travel and accommodation costs; and • Living expenses during the stay
 - Supporting documents may include: • Bank statements and financial records
 Submit biometric data (fingerprints and photograph) and supporting documents
 Ineligibility Conditions Applications may be refused if the applicant: - Intends to work, study, or live in the UK for extended periods
