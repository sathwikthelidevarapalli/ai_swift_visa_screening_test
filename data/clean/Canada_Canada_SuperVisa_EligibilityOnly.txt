 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must be a **parent or grandparent** of a Canadian citizen, permanent resident, or registered Indian
 Medical and Insurance Requirements - The applicant must complete an **immigration medical examination** by a panel physician approved by IRCC
 Financial Support Requirements - The Canadian child or grandchild must demonstrate that they meet the **Minimum Necessary Income (MNI)** based on family size, as defined by **Low Income Cut-Off (LICO)** guidelines
 - Acceptable proof of income includes: • Notice of Assessment (NOA) or T4/T1 tax documents
 Gather necessary documents, including proof of relationship (birth certificates), invitation letter, and medical insurance
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants may be found ineligible if they: - Do not meet financial or medical insurance requirements
 Legal References - Immigration and Refugee Protection Act (IRPA), Sections 11 and 39–42 — Requirements for temporary residents and admissibility
