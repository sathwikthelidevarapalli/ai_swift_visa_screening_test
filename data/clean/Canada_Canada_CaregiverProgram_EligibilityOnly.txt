 It consists of two main programs: - **Home Child Care Provider Pilot** - **Home Support Worker Pilot** These programs are designed to help Canadian families hire caregivers to provide essential support while offering workers a direct route to permanent residency after meeting certain criteria
 Basic Eligibility Requirements - Must **intend to live and work in Canada** (outside the province of Quebec) as a **home child care provider** or **home support worker**
 Work Experience Requirement - Applicants must demonstrate at least **24 months (2 years) of full-time work experience** in Canada in an eligible occupation within the past 36 months
 - Unpaid volunteer work does not count toward this requirement
 Education Requirement - Must have completed a **minimum of a high school diploma** (secondary education)
 Language Requirement - Must achieve a minimum of **CLB/NCLC Level 5** in all four language abilities: reading, writing, speaking, and listening
 Job Offer Criteria - Must have a **genuine and valid job offer** for a **full-time, non-seasonal** position in Canada
 - Relationship documents for accompanying family members (if applicable)
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants are ineligible if they: - Apply for employment in the province of Quebec
 - Fail to meet education or language test requirements
 - Provide fraudulent documents or job offers
