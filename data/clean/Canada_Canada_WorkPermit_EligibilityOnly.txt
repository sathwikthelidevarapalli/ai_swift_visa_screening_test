 Basic Eligibility Requirements - Must meet general eligibility conditions to work in Canada: • Obey Canadian law and have no criminal record (police certificate may be required)
 Eligibility for Applicants Inside Canada Applicants applying for a work permit **from within Canada** must meet at least one of the following criteria: - Hold a **valid study or work permit**
 Eligibility for Applicants Outside Canada Applicants outside Canada may apply for a work permit if they: - Have received a **valid job offer** from a Canadian employer supported by a positive **Labour Market Impact Assessment (LMIA)** (if required)
 - Meet all **admissibility requirements**, including background checks, medical exams, and financial sufficiency
 Eligibility for Applicants at a Port of Entry (POE) Applicants may apply directly at a **port of entry (airport or land border)** if they: - Are from a **visa-exempt country**, or - Hold an **Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA)** or other travel authorization permitting entry, and - Possess all required documentation, such as an LMIA approval, job offer letter, and proof of qualifications
 Medical and Security Requirements - Must undergo a **medical examination** if the applicant: • Intends to work in healthcare, childcare, or primary/secondary education
 - Extensions may be possible if the applicant continues to meet program requirements
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants may be found ineligible if they: - Work for an ineligible employer or prohibited business sector
 Legal References - **Immigration and Refugee Protection Act (IRPA)**, Sections 11, 20, and 200–205 — Requirements for temporary residents and work authorization
