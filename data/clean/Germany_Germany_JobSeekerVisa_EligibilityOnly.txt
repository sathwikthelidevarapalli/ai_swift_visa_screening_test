 It does not authorize employment but provides a legal stay for up to six months while searching for a job matching the applicant’s qualifications
 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must hold a **university degree** recognized in Germany or equivalent to a German higher education qualification
 - The applicant must meet general entry and security requirements under the **German Residence Act (AufenthG)**
 Educational and Qualification Requirements - The applicant’s foreign degree must be **recognized or equivalent** to a German higher education qualification
 Financial Requirements - Applicants must prove access to **sufficient financial means** to cover living costs during their job-seeking stay
 - If no employment is secured within six months, the applicant must leave Germany but may reapply later if eligibility continues to be met
 Gather all required documents (proof of funds, qualifications, health insurance, accommodation)
 - Proof of academic qualification (degree certificate and recognition proof)
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants may be found ineligible if they: - Fail to provide recognized proof of academic qualifications
 - Reapplication is possible after leaving Germany, provided eligibility continues to be met
