 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must have **completed a study program** at a **PGWP-eligible Designated Learning Institution (DLI)** in Canada
 Submit the required documents: proof of completion, study permit copy, passport, and recent photos
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants are **not eligible** if they: - Have previously obtained a Post-Graduation Work Permit
 Required Documentation - **Valid passport** and **current immigration documents** (study permit)
 - **Designated Learning Institution (DLI) details** confirming program eligibility
 - Incomplete or inaccurate documents
 - Work experience gained under a PGWP counts toward **Canadian Experience Class (CEC)** permanent residency eligibility
