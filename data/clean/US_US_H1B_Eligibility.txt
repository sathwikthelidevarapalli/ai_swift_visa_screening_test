 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must have a valid job offer from a U
 Employer files Form I-129 with USCIS, including the certified LCA, proof of qualification, and job offer
 Key Documents Required - Certified Labor Condition Application (LCA)
 - Employer’s support letter describing job duties, minimum qualifications, and offered wage
 - Passport copies and prior immigration documents (if applicable)
 Common Reasons for Ineligibility - Job does not meet the “specialty occupation” definition (no degree requirement or too general)
 - Applicant’s qualifications are not relevant to the position offered
 - Employer fails to obtain or comply with the certified LCA requirements
 - 20 CFR Part 655 Subpart H — Labor Condition Application Requirements
