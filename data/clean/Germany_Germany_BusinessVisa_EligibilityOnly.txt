 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The **applicant must be traveling to Germany for legitimate business purposes**, including: • Attending meetings, trade fairs, or conferences
 Sponsorship and Invitation Requirements - Applicants are often required to provide a **letter of invitation** from the host company or organization in Germany specifying: • Purpose of the visit, • Duration and location of business activities, and • Confirmation of financial responsibility (if applicable)
 Financial and Travel Requirements - The applicant must demonstrate **sufficient financial means** to cover travel, accommodation, and living expenses
 Prepare and submit required documents, including invitation letter, travel itinerary, and financial proof
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants may be found ineligible if they: - Fail to prove the genuine business purpose of travel
 - Frequent business travelers may apply for a **multiple-entry visa** valid up to 5 years if eligibility criteria are met
