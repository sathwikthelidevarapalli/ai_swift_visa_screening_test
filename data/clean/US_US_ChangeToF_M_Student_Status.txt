 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must **currently hold a valid nonimmigrant status** at the time of filing **Form I-539 (Application to Extend/Change Nonimmigrant Status)**
 - The school issuing the Form **I-20 (Certificate of Eligibility for Nonimmigrant Student Status)** must be **SEVP-certified** and authorized to enroll nonimmigrant students
 File **Form I-539 (Application to Extend/Change Nonimmigrant Status)** with USCIS, including supporting documents
 Key Documents Required - Form I-20 issued by SEVP-certified school
 - Copy of valid passport and prior immigration documents
 Common Reasons for Ineligibility or Denial - Failure to maintain lawful nonimmigrant status prior to filing
