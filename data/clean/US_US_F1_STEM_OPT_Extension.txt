 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The student must **currently hold valid F-1 visa status** and be in an authorized period of **post-completion OPT**
 Employer Requirements - The employer must be **actively registered in the E-Verify program** and remain in good standing
 Include all required documents and filing fee with the application
 Key Documents Required - Form I-765, Application for Employment Authorization
 Common Reasons for Ineligibility or Denial - Failure to file Form I-765 before expiration of current OPT
 - Students may not work for multiple employers unless each position meets all STEM OPT and training plan requirements
