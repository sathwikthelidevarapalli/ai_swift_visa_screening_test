 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The **applicant must be a family member** of a: • German citizen, or • Foreign national legally residing in Germany with a valid residence permit or settlement permit
 Language and Integration Requirements - The **spouse or partner** of the sponsor is generally required to demonstrate **basic German language proficiency (A1 level)** before visa issuance
 - Exemptions from this requirement may apply if: • The applicant is unable to learn the language due to health or other justified reasons
 Financial and Accommodation Requirements - The sponsor must have: • A **regular and sufficient income** to cover living expenses, including health insurance, rent, and food
 - Family members of German citizens typically receive **residence permits renewable indefinitely**, with eligibility for permanent settlement over time
 Submit required documents, including proof of relationship, accommodation, and income
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants may be denied a Family Reunion Visa if they: - Cannot prove a legitimate family relationship
 - Do not meet language requirements without valid exemption
