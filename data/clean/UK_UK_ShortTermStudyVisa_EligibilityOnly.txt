 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must be **accepted onto an English language course** at an **approved UK education provider**
 Financial Requirements - The applicant must demonstrate **sufficient funds** to cover: • Tuition fees for the entire duration of the course, and • Living expenses, accommodation, and return travel costs
 Health and Insurance Requirements - Applicants staying for more than 6 months must pay the **Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)** to access the National Health Service (NHS)
 Provide proof of enrollment, financial documents, and travel details
 Ineligibility Conditions The application may be refused if the applicant: - Does not provide proof of course acceptance at an approved institution
 - Time spent under this visa does **not count toward settlement** or future work visa eligibility
