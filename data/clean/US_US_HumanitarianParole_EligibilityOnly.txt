 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must be **outside the United States** at the time of filing the request
 - The final decision rests entirely at the **discretion of DHS**, and approval is not guaranteed even when eligibility is met
 Key Documents Required - Form I-131, Application for Travel Document
 Common Reasons for Ineligibility or Denial - Failure to establish an urgent humanitarian reason or significant public benefit
 - Discretionary denial by DHS despite meeting technical eligibility
