 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must have a **confirmed job offer** from a **Home Office–licensed sponsor** (a UK employer approved to hire foreign workers)
 - The offered position must fall under an **eligible occupation code** and meet the **required skill level** (RQF Level 3 or above, equivalent to A-level qualifications)
 - The applicant must meet the **minimum salary requirement**, which is the **higher of**: • The **general salary threshold** (£38,700 per year as of 2025), or • The **“going rate”** for the specific job as defined by the UK Home Office occupation codes
 Financial and Maintenance Requirements - Applicants must show evidence of **personal savings** of at least **£1,270**, held for **28 consecutive days** before applying, unless their sponsor provides maintenance
 Ineligibility Conditions Applications may be refused if the applicant: - Fails to secure a valid CoS from a licensed sponsor
 - Does not meet the salary or skill level requirements
 - Sponsors must comply with reporting duties and ensure the role continues to meet eligibility criteria
