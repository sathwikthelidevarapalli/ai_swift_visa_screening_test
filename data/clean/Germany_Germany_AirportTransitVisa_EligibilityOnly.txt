 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must be a **citizen of a country subject to the airport transit visa requirement** under the Schengen regulations
 - The applicant must possess **valid travel documents** for the final destination country and a **confirmed onward ticket**
 Exemptions from Visa Requirement Applicants are **not required** to obtain an airport transit visa if they: - Hold a **valid visa or residence permit** for a Schengen country, Andorra, Canada, Japan, San Marino, or the United States
 Submit all required documents (passport, flight itinerary, visa for final destination, proof of funds, etc
 Ineligibility Conditions An application may be refused if the applicant: - Does not belong to a nationality requiring an airport transit visa but applies incorrectly
 - Passengers with connecting flights through multiple Schengen countries must verify visa requirements for each airport
