 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The **applicant must possess an official admission letter** from a recognized German university or university of applied sciences
 - The applicant must **meet the academic entry requirements** applicable to the chosen program (such as prior qualifications or language prerequisites)
 Financial Requirements - Applicants are generally required to demonstrate access to **at least €11,208 per year (as of 2025)**, typically through a **blocked bank account**, scholarship, or sponsorship letter
 - The financial requirement ensures the applicant can support themselves without recourse to public funds
 Health and Insurance Requirements - The applicant must present proof of **comprehensive health insurance** valid throughout their stay in Germany
 Language and Academic Requirements - **Language proficiency** must be proven according to the study program’s requirements: • German-taught programs: Minimum **B1/B2 level** under the CEFR framework
 - Academic qualifications (transcripts, certificates, degree proofs) must be officially recognized as equivalent to German education standards
 Prepare required documents (proof of finances, health insurance, academic credentials, and language proof)
 Submit your visa application along with all required documents
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants may be denied if they: - Fail to provide proof of university admission or valid financial means
 - Do not meet language or academic requirements
