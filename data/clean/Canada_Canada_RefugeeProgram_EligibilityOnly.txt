 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must be **referred by a recognized referral organization**, such as the **United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR)**, or another **approved referral partner** recognized by Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC)
 Admissibility Requirements - All applicants must be **admissible to Canada**, which includes passing: • **Medical examination** to ensure they do not pose a public health risk
 Ineligibility Conditions - Individuals already recognized as refugees by another country and able to stay there permanently are not eligible for resettlement to Canada
 Evaluation Factors When reviewing eligibility, IRCC and referral organizations assess: - The **severity and credibility of persecution or risk**
 Legal References - **Immigration and Refugee Protection Act (IRPA), Sections 95–98** – Refugee protection definitions and eligibility
 - Resettled refugees may later apply for **Canadian citizenship** after meeting residency and other eligibility requirements
