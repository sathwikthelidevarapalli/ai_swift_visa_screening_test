uk/child-study-visa/eligibility Country: United Kingdom Visa Type: Child Student Visa 1
 It enables young students to receive quality education in a safe and regulated environment, provided they meet financial, parental consent, and institutional requirements
 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must be **between 4 and 17 years old** at the time of application
 Financial Requirements - The applicant must demonstrate sufficient funds to cover: • **Tuition fees** for the duration of the study program, and • **Living expenses** (boarding or accommodation) while in the UK
 Language and Academic Requirements - For primary and secondary education, **formal English proficiency testing is not mandatory**, but schools may conduct internal assessments
 Health and Insurance Requirements - The applicant must pay the **Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)** as part of the visa process
 Ineligibility Conditions An application may be refused if the applicant: - Fails to demonstrate adequate funding or parental consent
