 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The **applicant must be enrolled** in an **intensive German language course** consisting of at least **18 hours of instruction per week**
 Financial Requirements - The **applicant must demonstrate sufficient financial means** to support themselves during their stay in Germany, without the need for public assistance
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants may be found ineligible if they: - Fail to provide valid proof of enrollment in an intensive course
