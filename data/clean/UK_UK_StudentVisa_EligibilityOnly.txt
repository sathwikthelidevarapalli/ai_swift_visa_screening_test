 It replaces the former Tier 4 (General) Student Visa and enables students to pursue academic, professional, or vocational qualifications while maintaining lawful residence in the UK
 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must be **16 years of age or older** at the time of application
 - The applicant must meet all **immigration and health requirements**, including medical checks and biometric verification
 Financial Requirements - The applicant must show proof of funds to cover: • **Tuition fees** for the first year of study, and • **Living costs** (minimum amount set by UKVI depending on the city of study)
 English Language Requirements - The applicant must meet the **minimum English language standard** based on the level of study: • **RQF Level 6 and above (undergraduate or higher)** – B2 level on the CEFR scale
 Course and Sponsor Requirements - The course must be offered by a **licensed student sponsor** listed on the official **Register of Licensed Sponsors**
 Health and Immigration Requirements - Applicants must pay the **Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)** to access healthcare through the **National Health Service (NHS)**
 Submit biometric information and supporting documents
 Ineligibility Conditions The application may be refused if the applicant: - Fails to prove sufficient financial resources or course enrollment
 - Does not meet English language or academic entry requirements
 - Time spent under this visa may contribute toward future **Skilled Worker** or **Graduate Visa** applications but not direct settlement eligibility
