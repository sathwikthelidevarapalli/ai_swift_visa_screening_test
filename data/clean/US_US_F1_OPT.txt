 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must currently hold **valid F-1 visa status** and be enrolled in a full course of study at a **SEVP-certified school**
 Key Documents Required - Form I-765, Application for Employment Authorization
 Common Reasons for Ineligibility or Denial - Filing OPT application after the program completion deadline or beyond the 60-day grace period
 - STEM OPT extension requires compliance with employer reporting, training plans, and validation requirements
