 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must have a valid **Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS)** from a **UK-licensed sponsor** authorized to employ workers under the International Agreement route
 Financial and Maintenance Requirements - The applicant must show proof of financial means to support themselves for the first month in the UK unless their employer certifies maintenance on the Certificate of Sponsorship
 - Dependents (spouse/partner and children) may accompany the applicant if they meet the financial and eligibility criteria
 Ineligibility Conditions An application may be refused if the applicant: - Fails to obtain a valid Certificate of Sponsorship
 - Does not meet the age or financial requirements
