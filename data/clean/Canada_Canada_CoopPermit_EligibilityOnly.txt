 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must hold a **valid study permit** at the time of application
 Ineligibility Conditions Applicants are **not eligible** for a Co-op or Internship Work Permit if they are: - Enrolled only in **English or French as a Second Language (ESL/FSL)** courses
 - Enrolled in **general interest programs** or **preparatory programs** not linked to a degree, diploma, or certificate requirement
 - The Co-op Work Permit is issued at **no additional cost**, provided eligibility criteria are met
 - Incomplete or missing supporting documents
