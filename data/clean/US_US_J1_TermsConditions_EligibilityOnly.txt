 Admission and Duration of Status - J-1 exchange visitors are admitted to the United States for the **duration of their exchange program** as indicated on **Form DS-2019 (Certificate of Eligibility)**
 Departure and Program Completion - Exchange visitors must **depart the United States no later than 30 days** after the completion of their program unless they have obtained: • An approved **change of status** to another visa classification; or • A **waiver** of the two-year home-country physical presence requirement (if applicable)
 - Overstaying beyond the grace period results in **loss of J-1 status** and may affect future immigration eligibility
 Two-Year Home-Country Physical Presence Requirement (INA §212(e)) - Certain J-1 exchange visitors are **subject to a two-year home residency requirement**, meaning they must reside physically in their **home country for at least two years** before they become eligible to apply for certain U
 - The requirement generally applies to individuals who: • Received U
 - Affected participants **cannot change status** to an H visa, L visa, K visa, or apply for an immigrant visa until the two-year requirement is satisfied or officially waived
 - Exchange visitors must **maintain valid health insurance** meeting the minimum J-1 regulatory requirements
 - J-2 dependents are generally subject to the same home-country physical presence requirement as the principal J-1 exchange visitor
