 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must hold a **valid passport or travel document** that covers the entire duration of stay in the UK
 - The applicant must **apply from outside the UK** and **meet all immigration health and security requirements**
 Financial and Documentation Requirements - Proof of sufficient **funds** to cover travel, accommodation, and living expenses
 Submit supporting documents proving eligibility, purpose of visit, and financial stability
 Ineligibility Conditions Applications may be refused if the applicant: - Fails to prove intent to leave the UK after the visit
