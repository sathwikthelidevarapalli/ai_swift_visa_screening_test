 Overview The Humanitarian and Compassionate (H&C) Grounds program allows individuals who would not normally be eligible to become permanent residents of Canada to apply for an exemption from specific requirements of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act (IRPA) based on compelling humanitarian factors
 Basic Eligibility Requirements - The applicant must be **applying for permanent residence in Canada** or for a **permanent resident visa from abroad** on **humanitarian and compassionate grounds**
 - Applicants **already in Canada** may request consideration for an exemption from standard IRPA requirements if they can show that **exceptional circumstances** justify the request
 - The application must clearly explain why the applicant should be granted an exemption from the usual legal and regulatory requirements of immigration selection
