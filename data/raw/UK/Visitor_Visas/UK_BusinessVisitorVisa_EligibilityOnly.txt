Source: https://www.gov.uk/standard-visitor/visit-on-business
Country: United Kingdom
Visa Type: Business Visitor Visa (under Standard Visitor Route)

1. Overview
The UK Business Visitor Visa allows foreign nationals to visit the United Kingdom for short-term business-related activities without engaging in employment or long-term work.  
It is part of the **Standard Visitor Route**, designed for individuals attending meetings, conferences, negotiations, or training sessions on behalf of their overseas employer.  

The visa typically permits stays of up to **6 months** and is suitable for business professionals, company representatives, and entrepreneurs conducting legitimate short-term business engagements.

2. Basic Eligibility Requirements
- The applicant must satisfy the **general eligibility conditions** for a UK Standard Visitor.  
- The visit must be strictly for **permitted business activities** as defined by the UK Home Office.  
- The applicant must **intend to leave the UK** at the end of the visit.  
- The applicant must **not live in the UK for extended periods** through frequent or successive visits.  
- The applicant must **have sufficient financial means** to support themselves and any dependents without relying on public funds.  
- The applicant must **not seek employment or self-employment** in the UK during the visit.  
- The applicant must **apply from outside the UK** and **cannot switch visa categories** while in the UK.  

3. Permitted Business Activities
Visitors under this category may:  
- Attend meetings, interviews, seminars, or conferences.  
- Negotiate and sign contracts or business deals.  
- Attend trade fairs for business promotion (sales are not permitted).  
- Give one-off or short series of talks, provided they are not part of a commercial event.  
- Receive training from a UK-based company if it is required by their overseas employer.  
- Visit a UK site of their company or meet clients for business discussions.  

4. Financial and Documentation Requirements
- The applicant must demonstrate access to **sufficient funds** to cover:  
  • Travel and accommodation costs; and  
  • Living expenses during the stay.  
- Supporting documents may include:  
  • Bank statements and financial records.  
  • Employment letter or business invitation from the UK organization.  
  • Evidence of ties to home country (employment, family, or property).  
  • Valid passport with at least one blank page.  
  • Return travel bookings or itinerary.  

5. Restrictions and Prohibited Activities
Under this visa, the applicant **cannot**:  
- Take up employment or perform paid/unpaid work in the UK.  
- Establish or run a business as a self-employed individual.  
- Directly sell goods or services to the public.  
- Undertake a course of study (except short informal training).  
- Stay for more than **6 months** on a single visit.  
- Apply to extend or switch to another visa while in the UK.  

6. Application Process
1. Apply online through the **UK Government’s official visa portal**.  
2. Pay the **Standard Visitor visa fee**.  
3. Book an appointment at a **Visa Application Centre (VAC)** in your home country.  
4. Submit biometric data (fingerprints and photograph) and supporting documents.  
5. Await a decision from UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI).  

7. Validity and Duration
- The Business Visitor Visa is typically granted for **up to 6 months**.  
- Frequent business travelers may apply for **long-term multiple-entry visas** valid for 2, 5, or 10 years, with each visit limited to 6 months.  
- The visa does **not grant work rights** or lead to permanent residency.  

8. Ineligibility Conditions
Applications may be refused if the applicant:  
- Intends to work, study, or live in the UK for extended periods.  
- Fails to demonstrate sufficient funds or intent to return home.  
- Provides false or misleading documentation.  
- Has a previous record of immigration violations or overstays.  
- Poses a risk to UK security or public policy.  

9. Legal References
- **UK Immigration Rules Appendix V – Visitor**.  
- **Home Office Guidance: Visit visa – Business and Other Visit Categories (2024 Edition)**.  
- **Immigration Act 1971** and **Appendix V (Visitor Rules)**.  

10. Notes
- This visa category is suitable for **short-term business engagements only**.  
- Business visitors must **not receive payment** from a UK source except for reasonable travel and subsistence costs.  
- The visa must be applied for **before travel** if the applicant’s nationality requires it.  
- Activities outside the permitted business scope may result in **visa refusal, cancellation, or future bans**.
