Tourist Visa Application

Your guide to a smooth and enjoyable international travel experience.

How to Apply for a Tourist Visa

  1. Determine Eligibility: Check the specific requirements based on your nationality and destination country.

  2. Gather Documents: Collect all necessary documents as listed below.

  3. Complete Online Application: Fill out the visa application form accurately on our portal.

  4. Pay Fees: Submit the required visa application and processing fees.

  5. Schedule Interview (if required): Book an appointment at the embassy/consulate if an interview is necessary.

  6. Submit Application: Present your documents in person or submit them online/by mail as instructed.

  7. Track Status: Monitor your application status regularly through our portal.

  8. Receive Visa: Once approved, your visa will be issued!

Essential Documents for Tourist Visa

  • Valid Passport: With at least 6 months validity from your intended date of entry and 2 blank pages.

  • Visa Application Form: Duly filled and signed.

  • Passport-sized Photographs: Recent, colored, as per specific country guidelines.

  • Travel Itinerary: Confirmed round-trip flight bookings and accommodation details.

  • Proof of Funds: Bank statements, salary slips, or other evidence demonstrating sufficient funds for your trip.

  • Cover Letter: Explaining the purpose of your visit, duration, and planned activities.

  • Travel Insurance: Comprehensive travel insurance covering the duration of your stay.

  • No Objection Certificate (NOC): From employer/educational institution if applicable.
