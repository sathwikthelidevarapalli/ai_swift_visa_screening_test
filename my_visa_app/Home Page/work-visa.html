Unlock new career opportunities and professional growth abroad.

How to Apply for a Work Visa

  1. Secure Job Offer: Obtain a valid job offer or employment contract from a company in the destination country.

  2. Employer Sponsorship: Your employer may need to sponsor your work permit/visa application.

  3. Gather Documents: Collect all required personal, educational, and employment-related documents.

  4. Complete Online Application: Fill out the work visa application form accurately.

  5. Pay Fees: Submit the necessary visa and processing fees.

  6. Attend Interview (if required): Schedule and attend an interview at the embassy/consulate.

  7. Biometrics: Provide fingerprints and photograph as part of the application process.

  8. Await Decision: Processing times vary; ensure regular follow-up.

  9. Receive Visa: Upon approval, your work visa will be issued.

Essential Documents for Work Visa

  • Valid Passport: With sufficient validity and blank pages.

  • Visa Application Form: Duly filled, signed, and dated.

  • Passport-sized Photographs: Recent, colored, as per specific guidelines.

  • Employment Contract/Offer Letter: From the employer in the destination country.

  • Educational Certificates: Degrees, diplomas, and relevant transcripts.

  • Work Experience Certificates: From previous employers.

  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)/Resume: Updated and comprehensive.

  • Labor Market Opinion (LMO)/Sponsorship Proof: If required by the destination country.

  • Police Clearance Certificate: If applicable.

  • Medical Examination Certificate: If required.
