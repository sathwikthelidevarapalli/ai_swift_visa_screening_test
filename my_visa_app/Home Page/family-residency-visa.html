Family & Residency Visa Application

Uniting families and establishing long-term residency across borders.

How to Apply for a Family & Residency Visa

  1. Identify Visa Category: Determine the specific family or residency visa relevant to your relationship (spouse, child, parent).

  2. Sponsor Eligibility: Ensure the sponsoring family member meets all income and residency requirements.

  3. Gather Documents: Collect comprehensive personal, relationship, and financial documents.

  4. Complete Application: Fill out the detailed application forms for all applicants accurately.

  5. Pay Fees: Submit the necessary application and processing fees.

  6. Biometrics & Interview: Attend biometric appointments and interviews if mandated.

  7. Submit Application: Lodge your application with all supporting evidence to the relevant authority.

  8. Await Decision: Processing times for residency visas can be extensive; maintain communication.

  9. Receive Visa/Residency Permit: Upon approval, the visa or residency permit will be issued.

Essential Documents for Family & Residency Visa

  • Valid Passports: For all applicants, with sufficient validity.

  • Visa Application Forms: Duly filled, signed, and dated for each applicant.

  • Passport-sized Photographs: Recent, colored, as per specific guidelines.

  • Proof of Relationship: Marriage certificates, birth certificates, adoption papers, etc.

  • Sponsor's Documents: Passport, residency proof, income statements, tax records.

  • Proof of Accommodation: Housing arrangements in the destination country.

  • Medical Examination Certificates: For all applicants.

  • Police Clearance Certificates: For all adult applicants.

  • Proof of English/Local Language Proficiency: If required for permanent residency.
