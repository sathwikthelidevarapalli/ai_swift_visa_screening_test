Business Visa Application

Facilitating your global business ventures and professional engagements.

How to Apply for a Business Visa

  1. Confirm Business Purpose: Ensure your visit qualifies as a business activity (meetings, conferences, training).

  2. Gather Documents: Collect all necessary documents including invitation letters and company details.

  3. Complete Online Application: Fill out the business visa application form accurately.

  4. Pay Fees: Submit the required visa application and processing fees.

  5. Submit Application: Present your documents to the embassy/consulate or authorized center.

  6. Await Processing: Allow sufficient time for the visa processing, which varies by country.

  7. Receive Visa: Once approved, your business visa will be issued.

Essential Documents for Business Visa

  • Valid Passport: With at least 6 months validity and sufficient blank pages.

  • Visa Application Form: Duly filled, signed, and dated.

  • Passport-sized Photographs: Recent, colored, meeting specific guidelines.

  • Invitation Letter: From the host company/organization in the destination country.

  • Cover Letter: From your current employer, detailing purpose, duration, and financial responsibility for the trip.

  • Company Registration Documents: Evidence of your current company's legal status.

  • Travel Itinerary: Confirmed flight bookings and accommodation details.

  • Proof of Funds: Bank statements of individual or sponsoring company.
